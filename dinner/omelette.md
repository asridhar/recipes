# Omelette

Yes! For dinner because you can and you know you want to.

# Ingredients
* 3 organic brown eggs
* 1/2 Bell peppers
* 1/2 Onions
* Salt (to taste)

## Cooking Instructions
* Break eggs into a heated pan
* Add veggies
* Flip the omelette ike a boss
* Cook the other side for 30 seconds

## Serving Instructions
* With toast and hash browns
